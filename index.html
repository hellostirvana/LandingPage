About Stirvana

Stirvana is a food-tech platform where home chefs, food lovers, and event hosts connect, share recipes and culture. We make it easy to plan menus, book experiences, and automate groceries with AI. Starting first in Houston, Queens and Miami, we're building a trusted community marketplace for authentic food and culture experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will Stirvana launch?

We're launching in Spring 2026, starting with Houston, Queens (NYC), and Miami. Join the waitlist to be among the first to experience authentic home-cooked meals from talented local chefs!

Is this safe? How are chefs verified?

Safety is our top priority. All home chefs go through ID verification, background checks, and food safety reviews. Every chef has ratings and reviews from the community, so you always know who you're booking with.

How much will experiences cost?

Pricing varies by chef and experience type. Most home-cooked meals range from $15–35 per person, while private chef experiences and cooking classes vary based on scope. It’s authentic food at prices that respect both chefs and diners.

What cuisines will be available?

Stirvana celebrates the incredible diversity of Houston, Queens, and Miami! Expect authentic dishes from Vietnamese, Indian, Nigerian, Mexican, Filipino, Bangladeshi, Caribbean, and Middle Eastern cuisines — and many more. Our chefs bring heritage recipes passed down through generations.

Can I become a chef on Stirvana?

Absolutely! We're actively recruiting passionate home chefs who want to share their culinary heritage and earn income. Join our waitlist as a “Home Chef” and we'll guide you through onboarding. Founding chefs get zero commission for the first 90 days!

What does the AI do?

Our AI helps you plan menus, adjust recipes for your guest count and dietary needs, generate grocery lists, and even automate ordering. For chefs, it provides pricing guidance, menu optimization, and business insights. Think of it as your culinary assistant!
