About Stirvana

Stirvana is an AI-powered platform that connects Home Chefs, Community Hosts, and Cultural Explorers to create intimate dining experiences in real homes. The goal is simple: meaningful gatherings fueled by food, culture, and human connection.
  1. Explore Experiences: Browse curated cultural dinners and themed events.
  2. Choose Your Host: Select a Home Chef, community builder, or local cultural guide.
  3. AI Assistance: Stirvana AI helps plan menus, groceries, décor, and storytelling details.
  4. Attend or Host: Enjoy an authentic, intimate gathering designed for connection.
  • People who want to share their culinary heritage
  • Hosts who love creating community experiences
  • Travelers and newcomers seeking cultural immersion
  • Professionals looking for curated networking dinners
  • Anyone craving authentic food and meaningful conversation
Cultural dinners, networking gatherings, tourist immersion meals, community welcome dinners, themed events, and heritage-based storytelling nights.
Stirvana is preparing for a public launch in Spring 2026. Early waitlist members will get exclusive updates and first access to pilot experiences.

Hosts & Home Chefs

Stirvana supports a variety of host types:
  • Culinary Creators: Home chefs sharing authentic cultural dishes and traditions.
  • Social Weavers: Hosts who bring strangers and professionals together for connection and networking.
  • Local Guides: Storytellers who introduce visitors or newcomers to the city’s culture, history, and food.
  • Warm Neighbors: Community-minded hosts who welcome new residents and create neighborhood bonds.

Explorers (Guests)

Events may occur:

  • In the Explorer’s home (Chef-at-Home format)
  • In approved community or shared spaces
